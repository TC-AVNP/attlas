## Lessons

- Shopping sessions where the user is comparing products on Amazon require patience and going one item at a time — avoid dumping full hardware lists at once
- Three.js GLTFLoader has version compatibility issues when bundled with Vite — needs careful version pinning
- The user prefers to make hardware decisions incrementally, not be presented with a complete BOM upfront
