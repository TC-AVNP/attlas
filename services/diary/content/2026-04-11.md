## Lessons

- Per-repo git identity belongs in install.sh, not ~/.gitconfig. Global identity clobbers every other repo's intended author.
- Cloud Logging audit events are the wrong source for uptime. Guest-OS shutdowns, host maintenance, preemptions, and crashes are invisible to event replay. Cloud Monitoring's uptime metric is the ground truth.
- A 30-second cache on a frontend that polls every 15s is indistinguishable from no cache at all. Any external-API cache TTL must exceed the frontend's poll interval.
- Native SVG title tooltips are unusable for multi-dimensional charts: 2-second delay, no formatting, one segment at a time. Use a mousemove-driven div instead.
- Complexity accumulates quietly. main.go was 600 lines after Day 3 and 2000 lines by end of Day 4. Refactor discipline needs to be part of the plan, not an afterthought.
